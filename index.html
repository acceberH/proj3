Chipotle Bowl Recipe

Step1:

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 7 ounces of chipotle pepper in adobo sauce

Step2:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans, cooked
  • 1 pack of guacamole
  • 1/2 cup tomato salsa

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with all of these ingredients.

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken until fully cooked and then slice it.
  2. In a bowl, layer rice, black beans, lettuce(I don't like it so I didn't list it), and grilled chicken.
  3. Top with salsa and guacamole.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Chipotle Chicken Bowl!
